Variants 11–20 · Creative Explorations · Eurogulf Power Solutions

011 Propeller Abstract
Stylized three-blade propeller forms the counter of the G, linking marine engineering to the lettermark. Blades radiate from center with rotational energy.
012 Dual-tone Split
Each letter is horizontally bisected — top half in navy, bottom in copper. The split line creates visual tension and references engineering precision cuts.
013 Circuit / Technical
Letters constructed from PCB-style traces and junction nodes. Circuit pathways form the strokes with solder-point circles at corners. Engineering precision meets digital.
014 Maritime Deco
Art Deco geometric elegance meets maritime engineering. Tiered fan motifs, symmetrical framing lines, and a gold-on-navy palette evoke 1930s ocean liner luxury.
015 Brutalist Heavy
Ultra-bold slab forms with raw, unpolished edges. Massive letter weight dominates the frame. Concrete-grey palette channels industrial brutalism — pure structural power.
016 Modular Assembly
Letters built entirely from repeating square units on a grid — like precision-machined components assembled into form. Gaps between modules suggest engineered tolerances.
017 Horizon Line
Letters rise from a strong horizontal line — the horizon where sea meets sky. Lower portions fade into reflection below, evoking maritime vistas and Dubai's skyline silhouette.
018 Precision Instrument
A circular gauge/dial motif frames the monogram. Tick marks, a needle, and concentric rings recall ship engine instruments. Technical legibility meets premium finish.
019 Layered Depth
Three translucent overlapping copies of EGPS — offset diagonally — create depth through color mixing. Suggests multiple layers of engineering expertise and dimensional precision.
020 Signature Bold
Confident, hand-drawn quality with varying stroke weights — as if signed by a master engineer. Controlled imperfection over a precise baseline gives warmth to industrial authority.
