## 2025-2026 Logo Trend Research (Apply These)

### 1. Sharp Minimalism with High Contrast
Ultra-clean marks that read instantly at any size. Higher contrast, bolder presence. Mobile-first thinking — must work as favicon AND billboard.

### 2. Typography as Primary Brand Signal
Wordmarks taking center stage over icons. Custom type > generic fonts. Dropbox, Burberry direction — confident, not clinical.

### 3. Humanized Minimalism
Warm minimalism replacing sterile corporate. Slightly rounded corners, optical spacing, warmer tones. Simple but with personality.

### 4. Flexible Identity Systems
Logos that adapt, not just sit static. Think: how does this work across apps, social, print, signage? System thinking > single mark.

### 5. Bold Condensed Sans-Serifs
Heavy, compressed type with authority. Industrial DNA perfect for engineering.

### 6. Subtle Heritage Elements
Reintroducing craft details into clean logos. Think Burberry's return to heritage type — modern with roots.

### 7. Retro-Futurism
Chrome textures, engineered precision meets vintage industrial. Polished finishes on nostalgic forms.

### 8. Motion-Ready Design
Logos designed with animation potential built in. Parts that could move, reveal, assemble.

### 9. Ultra-Chroma Accents
Strategic use of vivid accent colors against controlled palettes. Electric gold, deep navy, industrial copper.

### 10. Reverse-Stress & Custom Type
Unconventional stroke weights, custom letterforms that own a space. Memorable through typographic uniqueness.
